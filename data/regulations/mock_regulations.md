# Regulatory Compliance Guidelines - Mock Data

## SEC Off-Channel Communications Rule
**Jurisdiction**: SEC (Securities and Exchange Commission)
**Rule Description**: All business-related communications must be conducted on approved, recorded channels. The use of personal text messages, WhatsApp, Signal, or other encrypted personal messaging apps for business purposes is strictly prohibited. Any hint of taking a conversation "offline" or to an "unmonitored channel" is considered a severe violation.
**Keywords**: "WhatsApp", "Signal", "personal phone", "off the record", "don't use email", "text me"

## FINRA Churning and Unsuitable Recommendations
**Jurisdiction**: FINRA (Financial Industry Regulatory Authority)
**Rule Description**: Brokers are prohibited from engaging in excessive trading (churning) in a customer's account simply to generate commissions. Trades must be suitable for the client's investment profile. Additionally, high-pressure sales tactics or unauthorized guarantees of returns are strictly prohibited.
**Keywords**: "guarantee", "sure thing", "no risk", "must sign today", "lose everything", "100% return"

## OFAC Sanctions Evasion
**Jurisdiction**: OFAC (Office of Foreign Assets Control)
**Rule Description**: Financial institutions must not process transactions involving individuals, entities, or countries on the Specially Designated Nationals (SDN) list or under comprehensive sanctions. Tactics to obscure the origin or destination of funds, such as shell companies in high-risk jurisdictions or rapid, unexplained wire transfers, must be flagged for review.
**Keywords**: "SDN", "sanctions", "obscure origin", "shell company", "high risk jurisdiction"

## Wash Trading Prohibition
**Jurisdiction**: General Market Abuse Regulations (e.g., FCA, SEC)
**Rule Description**: Executing trades in which there is no change in beneficial ownership (e.g., the same entity buying and selling the same asset simultaneously or within a very short timeframe) to create a false impression of market activity is prohibited.
**Keywords**: "wash trade", "same owner", "artificial volume"
